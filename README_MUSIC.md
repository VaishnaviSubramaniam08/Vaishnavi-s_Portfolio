# Background Music Setup

## Adding Music Files

To add background music to your portfolio:

1. **Place your music files in the public/music folder:**
   - `d:\portfolio-new\public\music\background.mp3` (primary format)
   - `d:\portfolio-new\public\music\background.ogg` (fallback format)

2. **Recommended music types:**
   - Ambient instrumental music
   - Lo-fi beats
   - Corporate background music
   - Soft electronic music

3. **File specifications:**
   - Duration: 2-4 minutes (will loop automatically)
   - File size: Under 5MB for faster loading
   - Format: MP3 (primary) + OGG (fallback)

## Music Player Features

- **Play/Pause Control**: Click the music button to toggle playback
- **Volume Control**: Slider appears when music is playing
- **Mute/Unmute**: Speaker icon to mute/unmute
- **Auto-loop**: Music repeats automatically
- **Responsive Design**: Works on all screen sizes
- **Browser Compliance**: Follows autoplay policies (user interaction required)

## User Experience

- Music button appears in bottom-right corner
- Visual feedback with animated icons
- Volume controls slide out when playing
- Smooth transitions and hover effects
- Respects user preferences (starts muted)

## Browser Compatibility

- Chrome/Edge: Full support
- Firefox: Full support  
- Safari: Full support
